Description: Build the internal administrative tools required to manage mosque employees, structure the leadership hierarchy, run committee elections, and operate the weekend Islamic school (Madrasa).
Key Features to Build:
1. Human Resource (HR) & Payroll Engine:

- Employee Profiles & Benefits: Create a system to store staff records (like Imams and facility workers), tracking their joining dates, educational backgrounds, and facility benefits (e.g., accommodation or food allowances).
- Payroll & Loan Tracking: Develop a payroll processor that tracks employee attendance and generates detailed payslips. It must also include a loan tracker that automatically highlights pending loan balances so administrators can deduct repayments directly from monthly salaries.
- Final Settlements & Certificates: Build workflows to calculate final settlements when an employee leaves, and generate standardized "Experience Certificates".

2. Governance & Election Management:

- Management Hierarchy: Create a module to document the official organizational structure, tracking the designations and tenures of the committee team (which is often required for Waqf Board authentication).
- Transparent Elections: Build a verified online voting system that handles everything from ballot creation to instant results, allowing the community to securely elect their management board.
- System Access Audit Logs: Implement a strict tracker that logs all system access and data entries by the management team to ensure ultimate internal transparency.

3. Islamic School (Madrasa) Management:

- School Administration: Because education is a core function of the mosque, you need a module to manage weekend or evening Islamic school programs.
- Academic Tracking: This should include features to manage student enrollments, class schedules, and homework assignments.
- Attendance & Parent Communication: Build tools to track attendance for both students and volunteer teachers, along with a communication portal to keep parents updated on their children's progress.
